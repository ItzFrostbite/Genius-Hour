What Is Genius Hour?

Genius Hour is a school project based on Google's 20/80 Policy were Google Lets there enijenneirs work on there own creations for 20% of their time. Genius Hour is a classroom activity that lets kids explore their passions/hobbies within bounderies made by the teachers. Genius Hour is a student-centered project that encourages creativity while building soft skills such as communication, creativity, teamwork and problem-solving that are nessersary for the scholars future.
